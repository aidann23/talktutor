OUR MISSION

IntervAI’s mission is to provide personalized, dynamic interview preparation that empowers students with the confidence, support, and tools they need to succeed, regardless of their background. By fostering a collaborative learning environment, we aim to bridge gaps in access and help every individual thrive in their career journey.

What is IntervAI?

IntervAI simulates live interviews, offering real-time feedback while allowing dynamic, fluid conversations. You can guide the conversation at any moment, and the AI will adapt effortlessly, helping you confidently prepare for any interview scenario. IntervAI references real-world interview data, industry trends, and commonly asked questions across various fields. It tailors your mock interview experience to reflect the most up-to-date practices, ensuring your optimal preparation.

OUR CORE VALUES

1. Equity in Education

“As a community college student, I was eager to grow and push my limits, but I quickly realized that I lacked the resources and support that others had access to. Watching students at four-year universities thrive with tools and guidance that weren’t available to me was frustrating. By leveraging IntervAI, I hope to bridge that gap and ensure students like me get the tools, confidence, and opportunities they deserve, no matter where they are.” — Dzinh Pham

2. Empathy Through Experience

“I personally faced the struggles of recruiting as I felt lost, overwhelmed, and anxious about the future of my career. Despite pouring hours into searching for resources, the process still felt confusing and isolating. I knew that there had to be a better way for all students walking this exact same path. By utilizing IntervAI, I intend to help others cultivate confidence in preparations for their next steps in life. ” — Tue Pham

3. Collaboration and Community

“I find collaboration as one of the most rewarding experiences and aim to extend it further than just creating a tool. My vision for IntervAI is to create an invaluable tool that not only helps individuals master their technical skills but also fosters a supportive community where others create more resources for one another. By contributing IntervAi I aspired to enrich a space where collaboration, growth, and shared success become part of the process.” — Aidan Nguyen

4. Purpose-Driven Innovation

“I have always thrived in dynamic, hands-on environments, which is why the static online resources often fall short of providing meaningful learning experiences. IntervAI breaks away from the rigidity of traditional study tools by combining a complex framework of adaptive logic with the capabilities of OpenAI. In hopes of helping revolutionize learning, we offer a richer, more interactive environment for all.” — Ben Pham
